Catholic Education and the Gonski Reforms: The Good, The Bad, The Ugly … And The Opportunities

The Gonski report has been hailed as the most comprehensive review of education funding in Australia for forty years. The political and economic environment means that all of the implications of the proposed funding reforms are not yet clear. This presentation will explore the implications of the Gonski review for the role of Catholic education in Australia and examine the leadership role of Catholic education and the NCEC in discerning viable funding proposals. There are many potential opportunities and challenges for Catholic education in the new school funding environment which will require dedicated leadership to navigate for the benefits of all students.

Mr Ross Fox, Executive Director, National Catholic Education Commission, AUSTRALIA
