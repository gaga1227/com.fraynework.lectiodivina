A coordinator for the Emerging Communities Resource Centre, Canterbury.I work for migrants indigenous people in Canterbury area. I visit South Pacific and Indigneous students in some of the Catholic schools in Canterbury areas, and also work with their families.

I am a member of the the advisory committee of the Canterbury council and work in the committees for the interfaith groups, multicultural groups and indigenous groups.

I have been a:

  • Teacher, assistant principal and principal in Catholic secondary schools in Tonga, Fiji and Samoa (for over 30 years)
  • A Director of Education for the Catholic schools in the Diocese of Tonga and Niue (1999-2003)
  • A tutor in the University of the South Pacific.
  • Co-facilitator for the Associate Certificate in Religious Education course for the teachers and leaders in Tonga under the Australian Catholic University, in 2003-2004.
  • A teacher and liaison for Tongan and South Pacific students and their families in some Catholic schools in New Zealand, Mildura and Robinvale, Victoria, and Sydney, New South Wales, Australia (for about 39 years).
  • A counselor and social worker for South Pacific families in Sydney.
  • A carer for children with disabilities in 2004-2012, in Sydney.
