Bio

Ms Judith Norris

Lecturer Educational Leadership
Australian Catholic University

Session Times

Judith currently teaches post graduate students in the educational leadership program at Australian Catholic University (ACU). Her work involves engaging with leaders and aspiring leaders in understanding the place of their values in their pursuit for ethical and moral leadership. Judith utilises coaching skills in her facilitation with post graduate students.

Specific research interests include accountability and ethical leadership - Judith's current doctoral studies - and investigating ways to enhance learning experiences for post graduate students in an online environment. Previous to Judith's work at ACU she held various leadership positions in both rural and urban settings in NSW and ACT at school and system levels.
