Elina Raso is Manager, Leadership and Professional Learning at the Catholic Education Office with responsibility for oversight of school improvement and accountability, leadership development, professional learning provision and National Partnerships.

A key focus of this role is working in partnership with schools to develop resources, support and information responsive to the varied professional learning needs of school leaders at different stages in their career journey.

Elina has worked across a range of areas in the Catholic Education Office Melbourne as a facilitator and leader. These include languages and literacy education, English as an additional language and system level professional learning provision.

Prior to this, Elina worked as a teacher and leader in primary schools, particularly those located in Low SES communities with significant proportions of non-English speaking background students.
