Discovering the “S” Catholic Educational Leadership for Mission and Hope; A System’S’ Approach.

The workshop will explore the dynamic required for 21st century Catholic Systems Leadership. Through a consideration of existing leadership frameworks and models the workshop will explore and communicate a contemporary hope filled dynamic for systems thinking. Using a metaphor that challenges leaders to think more holistically about system and systems, participants will engage in dialogue and active learning experiences to explore the contemporary metaphor.

Mr Michael Kearney, Director Administrative Services, Catholic Education Office Brisbane; Mr Tom Dorey, Principal St Mary’s College, Hobart; Dr Sandra Harvey, Head of School Services North, Catholic Education Office Tasmania; Ms Michelle Young, Senior Education Officer, Catholic Education Office Brisbane.
