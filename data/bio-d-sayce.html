As Director of Religious Education I have responsibility to the Bishops of Western Australia to develop and supervise the Religious Education program taught in their respective dioceses. In addition, I am responsible for the religious content presented in programs and activities.

As Assistant Director of Learning and Teaching, I am responsible for curriculum support. I am currently completing my doctorate in education at the University of Notre Dame Australia, Fremantle Campus. The research is in the area of beginning principalship and their professional needs.
