Since 2011, Anthony Gordon has held the role of Team Leader: Evangelisation and Religious Education, Catholic Education in the Diocese of Parramatta. Anthony has worked as the project manager of the Parramatta Religious Literacy Assessment since 2011, developing this online form of assessment in partnership with Patrick Nisbet from Creative Ministry Resources. Formerly, Anthony has enjoyed roles with CEO, Sydney as Archdiocesan Youth Ministry Coordinator and Faith Formation and Evangelisation Adviser. He has also worked as a Senior Lecturer in Theology & Chaplaincy Convenor at the University of Notre Dame Australia, Sydney. Anthony began his teaching career at Terra Sancta College, Schofields in the Diocese of Parramatta in 1999. He has a Master of Religious Education degree from the Australian Catholic University. Anthony is married and the father of one child.
