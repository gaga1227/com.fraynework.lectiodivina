I am a member of the Catholic Identity and Education Services Leadership Group and lead the Religious Education and Catholic Identity team in the Catholic Education Office Melbourne.

This group works with Religious Education Coordinators and teachers and with catechists and catechist coordinators developing contemporary approaches to learing and teaching in Religious Education. The group also works cooperatively with school leadership on developing the Catholic identity of schools. As part of the school improvement process, leadership teams are introduced to the Enhancing Catholic School Identity Surveys and invited to identify and explore ways to enhance the Catholic identity of their school in the light of the survey results.
