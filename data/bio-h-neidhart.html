Helga Neidhart, rsc is a Senior Lecturer in the Faculty of Education at Australian Catholic University. She holds a PhD from UNE and has degrees in Education, Arts, and Theology. Currently she teaches at Masters and Doctoral levels, supervises research and is active in personal research. Her research interests are in educational leadership, organizational development, spirituality and faith leadership.

Prior to taking up an appointment at ACU Helga was a secondary principal for many years. She is a Life Member of the Principals’ Association of Victorian Catholic Secondary Schools and has served on College Boards in several states.

She was Practitioner in Residence at the Principals’ Centre at Harvard University and was awarded Fellowships by both the Australian College of Educators and the Council of Educational Leaders. Currently she is serving on the Catholic Education Commission of Victoria.
