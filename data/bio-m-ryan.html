Archdiocesan Parent Community Educator CEO Sydney

Mary is currently employed as the Archdiocesan Parent Community Educator in the Catholic Education Office Sydney.

The role gives her the opportunity to work closely with school leadership teams and parents breaking down barriers of culture, class and education and empowering communities to work in collaboration in the interests of children. Mary works to build teacher and parent understanding of partnership and its impact on student learning and wellbeing. At a system level she is responsible for developing resources that support schools in engaging their communities.

Prior to moving into Parent Education Mary was a teacher of 22 years experience, most of these spent in Catholic Education in Sydney as a classroom and ESL Teacher K-6. She is currently completing a Masters in Educational Leadership at ACU.
