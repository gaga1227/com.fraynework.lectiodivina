Michelle Court is a Religious Education Adviser and Doctoral Student Researcher. She is a Religious Education Adviser for the Catholic Education Office, Sydney.

Previously she was the Religious Education Coordinator at a four stream Catholic primary school. Her teaching experiences led her to ask questions about leading the transformation of learning and learners in schools.

Her research is focussed on how explicit attention to moral purpose or the transformed learner influences teachers perceptions and practice in teaching, leading and learning.
