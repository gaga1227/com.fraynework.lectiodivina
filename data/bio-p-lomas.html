Bio

Mr Paul Lomas

Education Consultant
Catholic Education Office Sale, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Paul is one member of a team of four authors of this paper. Marianne O'Rourke, Anne Duncan and Dave Collie are also educational consultants in the diocese of Sale, Broken Bay and Wagga Dioceses. In his role as a Primary Education Consultant at the Catholic Education Office, Paul works to support principals and school staff to improve student achievement.

His work deliberates on assisting principals to focus on school improvement which draws together the key elements needed to build a healthy school culture and a strong organisation. He also works extensively within the diocese in the area of leadership development. His work work encompasses emerging school leaders as well as those engaged in a senior leadership. Paul has extensive experience within Catholic Education.

He has held four principalships across two dioceses ranging from a small rural school to foundation principal in the outer eastern growth corridor of Melbourne. His contribution to Catholic Education beyond the school setting includes representation on numerous committees at both a diocesan and State level.
