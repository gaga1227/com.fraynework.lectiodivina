Bio

Mr Ross Fox

Executive Director
National Catholic Education Commission, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Ross Fox began as Executive Director of the National Catholic Education Commission on 1 July 2013 leading the new commission which has been substantially reformed by the Bishops Commission for Catholic Education. Prior to his current position Ross was the Director of Policy Research and Communications at the Catholic Education Office Melbourne and Executive Officer of the Catholic Education Commission of Victoria Ltd.

Ross was educated at UNSW (Bachelor of Engineering, First Class Honours) and Oxford University (Honours MA in Philosophy, Politics and Economics). He has a depth and range of practical and consultancy experience in a diversity of situations – from McKinsey & Company working across Australia and New Zealand as a management consultant to remote Indigenous communities in WA where he was a relief store manager and a community development adviser. He has also worked in industry and as a senior political and policy advisor, and negotiator at state and federal level. He is active in local parish life and has a strong practical faith commitment.
