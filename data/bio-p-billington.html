Bio

Ms Philomena Billington

Director of Catholic Education
Catholic Education Office Sandhurst, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Phil is Director of Catholic Education in the Diocese of Sandhurst in Central Victoria. She has been in her 4th year in the role after 8 years as Deputy Director in Sandhurst with specific responsibility for Catholic Identity and Stewardship. She works closely with Diocesan church as well as education at diocesan, state and national level.

She has been a member of the National Catholic Education Commission (NCEC) Religious Education Committee, the Catholic Education Commission of Victoria (CECV) as well as other bodies and the ACBC Council for Disability.

Phil brings 40+ years experience as an educator and leader in Catholic and Government education across 5 dioceses and 4 states of Australia and has led curriculum design and development in two dioceses. She is passionate about education and Catholic schools, the Arts in education, parents as key leaders and lay ministry within our church.
