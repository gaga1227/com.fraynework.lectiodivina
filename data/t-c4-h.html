Chaired by Assoc. Professor Denis McLaughlin, Educational Leadership, Australian Catholic University

Building Identity Through Dialogue

This presentation will give an account of the work undertaken in the Archdiocese of Melbourne as part of the Catholic Eduvation Commission of Victoria Enhancing Catholic School Identity Project in conjunction with the Catholic University of Leuven. The assumptions behind the project, some major findings, and some subsequent developments will be presented.
