Associate Professor Scott Eacott

Associate Professor in Educational Leadership, Australian Catholic University

Session Times

Scott Eacott, PhD, is Associate Professor in Educational Leadership at the Centre for Creative and Authentic Leadership in the Faculty of Education at the Australian Catholic University. His research interests and contribution fall into three areas: theorising leadership; school leadership preparation and development; and reconceptualising strategy in education.

Scott leads a group of researchers investigating school leadership (see http://scotteacott.com) and is currently completing a book entitled 'Relational administration: a theory and methodology for educational leadership' to be published by Sense. Email: Scott.Eacott@acu.edu.au
