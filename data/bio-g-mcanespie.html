Mrs Gabrielle McAnespie

Principal
St Charles Primary School, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Gabrielle McAnespie has been a class teacher, REC, AP and Principal in Independent and Catholic Systemic Schools for thirty three years. Her special interests include change management in schools and systems, early learning, practitioner enquiry and Aboriginal perspectives.

Gabrielle is currently Principal at St Charles' Primary School at Waverley which is located in Sydney's Eastern Beaches. She served as the primary principal representative on the Sydney Archdiocesan Catholic Schools Board (SACS Board) for 5 years, held positions in the Sydney Catholic Primary Principal Association (CPPAEast) over ten years and was the elected inaugral Eastern Region representative on the Archdiocesan Primary Principals' Executive Committee.
