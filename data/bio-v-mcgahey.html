Dr Vicky McGahey

Lecturer
Australian Catholic University

College, Warriewood, NSW. In 2011 Vicky completed a Masters degree of Theology at the University of Newcastle. A high school teacher of Religion, Science and Music. Coordinator of Science, and Creative and Performing Arts. Vicky is also a past Director of Human Resources. She has written a young adult fiction book and a song based on the findings of her Doctorate research on leadership attributes (qualities). Presently, working on marketing "The Leadership Attributes Game" as devised from the same research.

An active member of her local community and parish as a Rotarian and an Officer of Cadets. Vicky is a singer and a song writer presently recording her work. Interests include leadership theory and practice, theology politics, the Australian bush, scuba diving, reflection and writing.
