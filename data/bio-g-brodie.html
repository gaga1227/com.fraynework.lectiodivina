Bio

Mr Geoffrey Brodie

Director of Mission
St Patricks College Ballarat, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Currently Director of Mission at St Patrick's College, Ballarat, a Catholic boys' school in the Edmund Rice Tradition. It currently has an enrolment of over 1300 students.

Taught in the senior school for 17 years and previously held the position of Head of Humanities. Obtained a Master of Arts from the Australian Catholic University.

Interest in the work of Bernard Lonergan SJ and its application to education in a secondary context. Presented at the Victoria Commercial Teachers' Association (VCTA) annual conference on four ocassions. Married to Therese with eight children, three of which have no interest in Bernard Lonergan.
