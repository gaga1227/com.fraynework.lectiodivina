Jayne-Louise has extensive experience in Catholic Education, including leadership roles in schools and within the central and regional education offices of the Catholic Education Office Melbourne. Her most recent experience has been the co-leadership of a professional inquiry project, Leading for Contemporary Learning in Catholic Schools.

The project aims to enable leadership capacities across the system with a focus on developing deep understandings of contemporary approaches to teaching and learning within the context of Catholic learning communities.

Jayne-Louise is also a member of EdPartnerships International where the focus of her work is with school leadership in enabling partnering to learn opportunities that foster excellence in student learning. Currently Jayne-Louise is a doctoral student at Australian Catholic University where she is undertaking research with a focus on system capacity building.
