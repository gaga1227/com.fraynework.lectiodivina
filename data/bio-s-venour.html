In my role of Curriculum Initiatives I have the opportunity to review the needs of our students, current best practice and the changing needs in education to propose and new implement new ideas and initiatives to the College. As a part of this role I have overseen the implementation of new ICT structures at the college, and a range of new subject areas.

A greater part of the role has focussed on planning and running much of the Professional Learning at the College. In order to develop a Professional Learning program that provided more opportunities for teachers to collaborate, share ideas and learn new skills.The new model has allowed a greater emphasis on Professional Learning and shared understanding across the College.

Previous roles at the College include Assistant to the Principal- Pastoral care, Special Education, English, Humanities and Social Sciences and Literacy Coordinator. In these roles my focus has always been the development of curriculum to increase the educational outcomes of our students.
