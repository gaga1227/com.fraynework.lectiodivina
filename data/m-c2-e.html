Reflective Evaluation for Active Leadership

Reflective Evalution for Effective Leadership (REAL) is a program that has been offered to principals in Catholic Schools since 2011. The process is intended to be supportive, encouraging and nurturing to the principal in their professional growth. It strongly aligns with the Australiana Teacher Performance and Development Framework (August 2012).
