Professor of Identity and Curriculum, Australian Catholic University, McAuley Campus, Brisbane. Adjunct Senior Lecturer, Department of Education and Professional Studies, University of Limerick, Ireland.

Worked as a post-primary teacher in Ireland before becoming a teacher educator working on both consecutive and concurrent programmes. Main areas of research interest include curriculum development, reform and evaluation;curriculum and education policy; development education; initial vocational education and training; gender studies. Nominee of Irish Universities on Teaching Council of Ireland (2005-2012). Education Officer National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (1993-1996).
