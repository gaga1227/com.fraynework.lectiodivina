Dr Paul McQuillan

Coordinator of Research
Catholic Education Office Brisbane, AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Dr Paul McQuillan is Coordinator of Research with Brisbane Catholic Education and a practising family and relationships counsellor with Anglicare South Queensland.

He is an Honorary Fellow of Australian Catholic University (2002 to present).

His current activities include:

  • Member of the International Seminar on Religious Education and Values. The seminar meets bi-annually and is restricted to members and invited guests.
  • Chair Education Program Accreditation Committee of the Psychotherapists and Counsellors Federation of Australia. The role of the committee is to accredit training in psychotherapy and counselling provided by Universities and other training organisations.
  • Editorial Board of Panorama: Intercultural Annual of Interdisciplinary Ethical and Religious Studies for Responsible Research
  • Editorial Board of The Journal of Youth Ministry

His research interests include:

  • Religious experience and the spirituality of youth. (Original PhD research)
  • Meaning centred therapy and the logotherapy of Viktor Frankl (Psy D research)

His previous career has centred on education and educational leadership:

  • Director, Administrative Services – Brisbane Catholic Education (12 years)
  • Assistant Director, Resources and Planning – Northern Territory Catholic Education
  • Principal of High Schools in three states

Dr Paul McQuillan is the author of numerous journal articles, book chapters and some books on the topic of spirituality and belief systems of young people.
