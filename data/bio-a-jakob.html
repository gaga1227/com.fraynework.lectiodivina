Annette Jakob is currently the Chair of St Mel's Primary School in Shepparton, Victoria. She has been active on the St Mel's Parish Education Board as a parent representative from 2008, Vice Chair2009-2010 and Board Chair from 2011 to current. In 2012, Annette was nominated as the Sandhurst Schools Education Board Parent Representative for the Board Chairs of the Goulburn Valley Deanery, along with three other parent representatives. The role involves active participation and parent engagement/conusltation in decisions of policy and strategic directives for Catholic Edcucation, Sandhurst Diocese, Victoria. Of course, Annette is a mother of two boys: one in secondary school and one in primary school.

Annette is a qualified and registered Occupational Therapist, acquiring a Bachelor of Applied Science (Occupational Therapy) at LaTrobe University and has further qualification: Post Graduate Diploma in Ergonomics from LaTrobe University and Cdertificate IV in workplace training and assessment. She currently runs her own private consulting business in Occupatioanl Therapy, Occupational Health and Safety and Ergonomics.
