Rosie Phillips and Angela Pope both commenced at St Thomas’ as graduate teachers and quickly became respected and high performing teachings working together in an open plan classroom. Last year both Rosie and Angela completed Phase Two of the Victorian Institute Of Teaching’s High Performing Quality Teacher Program. They both assist one another to create a positive learning culture not only in their classroom but within the school environment.

Jason Slattery has been a principal for ten years within two school settings. He strives to create a positive learning environment and sees the importance of allowing his teaching staff to challenge themselves in professional development. Jason loves working with children and sees his role as principal as being a privilege.
