Session Times

Ian is currently Assistant Principal at Sancta Maria College in Auckland, New Zealand. He has been involved in education for over 29 years in England, Australia and for the last 14 years in New Zealand.

As a Mathematics specialist he has taught in High Schools from Years 7 -13, including Scholarship, been Head of Mathematics once leading a team of 28 teachers and was a School’s Examinations Officer for 5 years. Before leaving England, he was a member of the Senior Leadership at Glyn School in Surrey.

In his current position as an AP, he has a major role to play in providing a caring school community and has instigated a range of initiatives to support the wellbeing of the teachers. He has presented his teacher wellbeing seminar in numerous schools and at conferences in New Zealand and is the author of the 'Good New Habits' resource to support teacher wellbeing in schools.
