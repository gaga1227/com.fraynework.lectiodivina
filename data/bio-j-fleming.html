Associate Professor Joe Fleming

Session Times

I taught in Catholic Schools in Victoria for almost 25 years. After that I held a number of positions at the Catholic Education Office Melbourne: Manager of Religious Education; Senior Adviser Innovation and Projects.

I was also a Lecturer in Religious Education at Australian Catholic University (Melbourne Campus) and now Campus Dean at the Ballarat Campus. In 2001 I completed a PhD focusing on the role of Religious Education Coordinators in Catholic Secondary Schools.

In 2002 I was awarded a scholarship to attend the Bat Kol Institute in Jerusalem to study the Torah. In 2006 I was the Cardinal Basil Hume Visiting Scholar at Cambridge University UK where I undertook post doctoral studies in Catholic Identity.
