John Edwards MEd (Hons) is a statistical analyst with over 20 years’ experience teaching in secondary schools and 17 years working with the Catholic Education Office – Sydney. He has lectured at the University of Western Sydney in the theory of education and research methodology. He is a member of the Statistical Society of Australasia and is Director of Riley, Duncan and Edwards Workplace Bullies Research.

In 2005, 2007 and 2012 he was part of a research team with Professor Deirdre J Duncan (ACU) and Dr. Dan Riley (UNE) investigating workplace bullying in Australian schools. In 2009, with Riley and Duncan, he published An Investigation of Workplace Bullying in Australian Schools, the first national study of workplace bullying in schools; this was followed up in 2012 with The Bullying of Staff in Schools (ACER, 2012).

Apart from social research into workplace bullying he is also a published author in the area of values education, publishing Religious Education in a Pluralist Society – the Key Philosophical Questions, Peter R Hobson & John S Edwards (Woburn 1999); currently he is working on a sequel to this book and a further book entitled Towards a Philosophy of Catholic Education.
