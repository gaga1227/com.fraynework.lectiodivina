Peggy Saab, Regional Consultant, Sydney Catholic Education Office, Doctoral Student Researcher She has been a Catholic primary school for twenty-five years, in three very different school settings. The size, the demographics and the knowledge and skills of her colleagues have been rich and diverse.

Her representation of principals on local, state and national levels provides another dimension to her understanding of the challenges in primary schools. She was a member of a school-based core team which developed a model of leading for learning.
