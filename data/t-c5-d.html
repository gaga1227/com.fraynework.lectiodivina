How Tight Processes Challenge Practices of the Past to Create a Meaningful and Dynamic Professional Learning Culture.

As educators, we are continually aware of the need to be open to continuous learning. Learning is a process that has neither a distinct beginning nor end but is an ongoing journey that can reenergize, ignite new ideas and allow us to grow both professionally and personally. The challenge for us is to work within a culture that demands more administrative work on the part of teachers, often removing the focus from being the best we can in the classroom.

This practical workshop will share the journey of one school, sharing practical ideas as to how to manage change in order to develop a strong culture of shared practice and professional learning.

Mrs Sonia Venour, Assistant to the Principal - Curriculum Initiatives, Our Lady of the Sacred Heart College
