Leading with Grace: The Shepherd Metaphor for Leadership

Simplicity is one of the hardest constructs to grasp, produce and maintain in any human endeavour. This also applies when writing on leadership for the formation of communities within business, organisations and families.

Simplicity is a paradox when we think of the complexity of our lives. Many solutions and answers to problems can be found in the simplicity of a few words, a simple good deed or simply the gift of time we give to ourselves in contemplation.

Dr Vicky McGahey, Lecturer and Teacher of Religion and Science, Australian Catholic University and Mater Maria College
