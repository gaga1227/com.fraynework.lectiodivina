Session Times

As Executive Director of Australian Institute for School Governance I am responsible for providing a range of services for school leaders, school boards/councils, and school owners or system administrators. These services include governance workshops, conversations, publications, research, and advocacy.

Prior to my current role I have been a school governance consultant for six years in two educations systems. In addition, I have been principal of an independent school.

I am a member of CRV Education Governance Working Party; member of AICD; certificated member of CSA, and member of ACEL.
