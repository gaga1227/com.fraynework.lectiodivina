I am currently a PhD student at the Australian Catholic University in the field of Educational leadership and management which I first encountered at Rhodes University, South Africa.

I have been lecturing in educational leadership, school management and research methods for undergraduates students. My research interests are social justice issues & inequalities, female leadership, and school leadership for community-building.

My PhD research project explores the perceptions and strategies of an inclusive education for social justice in low SES schools of Mauritius.
