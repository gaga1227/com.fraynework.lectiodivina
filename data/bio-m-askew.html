Dr Mark J Askew is currently Head of Educational Services, Catholic Schools Office, Diocese of Broken Bay. Mark began his teaching career as a teacher of English and History, and has worked in curriculum development and program management roles with the Sydney Catholic school system and the NSW Catholic Education Commission.

He has a passion for the idea of Catholic curriculum and the Catholic Worldview, and excellence in teaching and learning. In September 2009 he was seconded from this position for nine months to work as Curriculum Manager with the Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority.
