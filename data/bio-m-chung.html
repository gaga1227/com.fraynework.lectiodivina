Bio

Mrs Marie M Gilberte T Y Chung Kim Chung

Director, Bureau of Catholic Education, MAURITIUS

Session Times

Gilberte Chung is the Director of Catholic education in the Diocese of Port-Louis in Mauritius since 2007. She has been a secondary school teacher, a Deputy Principal and a Principal in four Loreto secondary schools.

She was also Adviser at the Ministry of Education in 2005-2006, and was responsible for the Policy and Strategy Paper on Special Educaion Needs. Holder of a BA and MA in French, as well as a PGCE, she completed an MEd Leadership with the Australian Catholic University in 2004. She is presently an EdD Doctoral student with Liverpool Hope University in UK.

She was awarded Chevalier dans l'Ordre des Palmes Academiques for the promotion of education and culture in 2010. In the education sector in Mauritius, she is known for her stand and contribution for the right to education for all children and the work being done for the most vulnerable. As examples of innovations and creativity in the catholic education sector, there are namely the ecological literacy programmes and green school projects for sustainable education, the encouragement to use the differentiated teaching and learning pedagogy, the bilingual programme in the prevocational sector and the promotion of the use of mother-tongue in the early years.
