Developing a Leadership Team Charter

In 2010 the Gleeson College Leadership Team developed a team charter based on the L5 Frame and the four Dimensions of Leadership from Catholic Education S.A. In 2012 the team commenced a review of the charter in line with current educational leadership research. In 2013 the Leadership Team will work with Professor Neil Cranston to further refine the final draft of the revised charter.

Our presentation will outline the processes and protocols used by the team and highlight the amazing benefits of embarking on such a leadership journey together. We will also share the practical outcomes we achieved for the team and the College but please remember that the journey never ends!

Mr Philip Lewis, Principal, Gleeson College
