Bio

Mr Chris Kitching

PhD Candidate and University Tutor
University of Notre Dame (Sydney), AUSTRALIA

Session Times

Chris is completing a fulltime PhD in the Laicisation of Leadership in Australian Jesuit schools at the Unveristy of Notre Dame Australia. His is also a sessional tutor in the School of Education. He has experience of twentyfive years of teaching and held numerous Executive, academic, pastoral and co-curricular positions in independent schools. His most recent appointment was a founding Director on the Executive Team of Saint Ignatius' College, Riverview.

During this time he developed a great interest in the role of laity in partnership with the Jesuit Fathers leading their schools. A particular focus of his interest has been organisational change, culture and climate and leadership in education. In 2010, Chris undertook a fulltime Master of Business Adminsitration (MBA) at the Macquarie University Graduate School of Management. He has also completed a Master in Educational Adminsitration.
